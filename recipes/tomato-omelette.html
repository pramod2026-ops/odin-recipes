Tomato Omelette

Photo by Raamin ka on Unsplash

Simple and Protein-Rich Breakfast

Tomato omelette is a quick and nutritious dish made with eggs and fresh tomatoes. It is perfect for a healthy breakfast or light meal.

This recipe is very easy and can be made in under 10 minutes. You can also add onions, chilies, or herbs for extra flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small tomato (chopped)
  • 1 small onion (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter

Steps:

  1. Beat the egg in the bowl.
  2. Add chopped tomato, onion, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat oil or butter in a pan.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  5. Cook on medium heat until set.
  6. Flip and cook the other side.
  7. Serve hot.
