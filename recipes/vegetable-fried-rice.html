Vegetable Fried Rice

Photo byKalyani Akella on Unsplash

Quick and Flavorful One-Pan Meal

Vegetable fried rice is a simple and delicious dish made with leftover rice, fresh vegetables, and basic seasonings. It's popular across many Asian cuisines and is perfect for a quick lunch or dinner.

This dish is highly customizable—you can use any vegetables you have at home like carrots, peas, or capsicum. It's beginner-friendly and takes less than 30 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 green onions (chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 green onions (chopped)

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok.
  2. Add chopped garlic and saute for a few seconds.
  3. Add vegetables and cook until slightly soft.
  4. Add cooked rice and mix well.
  5. Pour soy sauce, add salt and pepper.
  6. Stir-fry for 3-5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.
